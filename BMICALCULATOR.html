About us

Founded in 2021, Ashaka Education Pvt. Ltd. is committed to delivering high-quality education to students across rural India. We offer foundational courses in Commerce, Arts, English, and Computer Science, and practical online programs like our 60-day On-the-Job Training (OJT) to build real-world skills.

Our mission

Provide urban-level learning experiences to rural learners through accessible content, mentorship, and hands-on projects.

Our approach

Structured curricula, project-based learning, and continuous feedback—so students can learn, build, and showcase.
